Extra Blog 1! When you own rats, it is very, very easy to accidentally collect more rats. Whenever I see an animal rescue post on Facebook, my brain tends to static out and I resume consciousness with the animal in hand. I'm only able to beat back the mesmerization when I don't already have the enclosure for the animal on hand; this means I'm able to resist the temptation of reptiles, amphibians, and other rodents, because I'm not able to make space for them with the aquariums and cages I have on hand. But rats? I can just keep getting rats. Specifically, I can get up to seven of each sex of rat. Last week, I had four of each. This week, I have five each, for a total of ten. I have strayed into the treacherous territory of double-digit rat ownership.
