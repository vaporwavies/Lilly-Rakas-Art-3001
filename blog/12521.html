What happened to Internet Art?

Over anything else, one observation has stood out to me as I've attempted to gather internet artworks
for the are.na assignment.

New works are *stunningly* difficult to find.

The artists that I'm familiar with that I've considered staples in the genre, have slowed their
output to a trickle, or a dead halt.

Has the novelty of the internet worn off? Is this a failure on my part, or on hosting platforms'
ability to disseminate this kind of artwork?

In a time when the internet has become so crucial, why does this medium seem so dead?
